Weekly Challenge: Souper Fall Showdown

Overview:

We invite culinary enthusiasts to create and submit their most delicious, autumn-inspired soups. This seasonal challenge celebrates the flavors of fall, featuring ingredients like pumpkins, squashes, root vegetables, hearty greens, and warming spices. Whether it's a creamy pumpkin bisque, a savory butternut squash soup, or a hearty lentil stew, participants are encouraged to experiment with their favorite autumnal ingredients to craft the perfect comforting bowl.

Ingredients:

Each soup should feature at least one seasonal fall / autumn ingredient as its main focus (e.g., root vegetables, squash, fall herbs). Creativity is encouraged, but the recipe should embrace the spirit of fall.

Reward:

The three winners (as voted by the community) will appear as "Chef of the Week" and receive a shout-out! The user's profile and submission will appear on our social media alongside their placement rank.
